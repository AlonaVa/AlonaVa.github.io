Summer film festival

Watch it outside!

When: August 5th - 8th, 2020

Where: Boston Public Garden

“Watch it outside” is the selection of the best movies of the XX century. Our film festival taking place every august in Boston from 2015. “Watch it outside” is your opportunity to see your favourite old movies in a new light! Festival can hold up to 1,500 people. Shows are free and open to everyone, but number of tickets is limited, so book your ticket now!

HOT NEWS

Film Festival should open on August 5 despite Covid-19 pandemic, but please don’t forget to bring your own face mask and use the hand sanitizers that would be available!
